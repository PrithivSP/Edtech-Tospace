What is ToSpace?

ToSpace is the first company in India to run a satellite kit space programme. This course will be taught by specialists who are currently working on live satellite projects. Every student enrolled in our course is required to build a CubeSat before the end of the programme. Even a pupil with no prior knowledge of space can build a satellite. This is the level of instruction we provide to pupils in order to educate them about the space industry.
